Overview Screenshots Reviews

Ashen Dominion drops you into the dying kingdom of Veyl, a fractured open world where every ember of civilization clings to the last warm hearths. As a Cinderbound wanderer, you carve your own path through ruined citadels, plague-choked forests, and the throne rooms of mad kings.

Combat is deliberate and punishing — read every enemy, manage your stamina, and earn each victory. A deep affinity system lets you fuse weapon arts with elemental relics for thousands of build permutations, while seamless drop-in co-op means a friend is never more than a summoning sign away.

With over 80 hours of handcrafted content, branching faction questlines, and an original orchestral score, Ashen Dominion is the studio's most ambitious world to date.
